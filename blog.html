A Journey Inwards



They say a picture speaks a thousand words, this picture, right here, sums up the journey I undertook for an intense 21 weeks, the Isha Hatha Yoga teacher training, which enabled me to become a much more enabled version of myself which I never knew existed. Trained under the direct guidance of enlightened master and Guru, Sadhguru Jaggi Vasudev, we at Yoga with Kush are offering Hatha Yoga in its classical form.

We offer Isha Hatha Yoga modules and packages that are designed by Sadhguru. Our aim is to bring inner wellbeing and health to individuals and corporates.
