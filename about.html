An Undercover Yogi

Yoga With Kush is founded, managed and run by Kushendra Maya Tiwary and is the first private yoga studio in Jharkhand,India to teach Isha Hatha Yoga.

Kushendra undertook the internationally-renowned 1750-hour Isha Hatha Training Program in South India in 2018, wishing to bring Hatha Yoga in its full depth and profundity to an Australian audience.

Also a graduate of Jamia Millia Islamia, life has offered him a range of conventionally rewarding career pursuits that he has ignored in the pursuit of yoga.
